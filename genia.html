Health Risks of Being Overweight

Being overweight can lead to serious health complications such as:

  • Heart disease and strokes
  • Type 2 diabetes
  • High blood pressure
  • Sleep apnea
  • Fatty liver disease
  • Kidney disease
  • Joint and muscle pain

It is important to maintain a balanced diet, exercise regularly, and consult healthcare professionals to manage weight effectively.
